Level of Engagement

Our group was able to receive great feedback from the viewers of our stream. For most they enjoyed the entirety of the stream. They enjoyed our UNO game mostly since it was chaotic and it was really fun even for us due to the constant laughter. Our audience also enjoyed that we were constantly engaging and arguing with each other for fun and interacting a majority of times in a more silly, goofy, joke-y, and raw manner. The other parts of the stream were all fairly liked in terms of balance, which most likely due to the more fun "Unscripted" and "Niche" feel and vibe of the stream as well as the people involved (streamers) being generally likeable throughout, During the stream our viewers were also interacting directly with our group via allowing joins or linking the servers this gave our audience the feel that were also playing with us. We were able to receive a total of 202 messages throughout the session from our viewers.



Viewership

Our stream lasted 2 hours and 30 minutes, with an average of 7 viewers and a peak of 13. We had 22 unique viewers, 9 unique chatters, 59 live views, and gained a follower that day. 40% of our audience are at the age of 15, 33.4% are 16, and the remaining 26.7% are 17 years old. Additionally, 80% of our audience are female. Based on our feedback form, 53.3% of viewers rarely watch streams, which explains why some did not stay long. Most of the viewers found our stream through friends (80%), classmates (53.3%), and promotions (26.7%).
